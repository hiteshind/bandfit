Nutrition • Ovo-Vegetarian Meal Plan

7-Day High-Protein Meal Plan (41+)

~2800–3200 calories | 180–220g protein daily

Day 1 (Training Day)

Breakfast: 3-egg omelette + spinach + cheese + Greek yogurt
Post-Workout: Protein shake + banana + peanut butter
Lunch: Paneer + quinoa bowl with veggies
Dinner: Lentil dal + rice + curd + salad

Day 2

Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with nuts + boiled eggs
Lunch: Chickpea salad + paneer + avocado
Snack: 2 boiled eggs + almonds
Dinner: Vegetable stir-fry with tofu + brown rice
Days 3–7 follow similar structure with variations: eggs, paneer, lentils, dairy, nuts, and whole grains.

Quick Vegetarian Protein Combos

  • Rice + Lentils (Dal-Rice)
  • Eggs + Whole Grain Toast
  • Paneer + Vegetables
  • Greek Yogurt + Nuts
  • Quinoa + Chickpeas
  • Peanut Butter + Banana
